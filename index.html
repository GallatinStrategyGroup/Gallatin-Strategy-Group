Our Approach

Two proprietary channels. Zero wasted spend.

Channel 01

AI-Driven Targeting

Our proprietary algorithm cross-references school-level SAT/ACT performance, household income, median home values, and counselor-to-student ratios to map the highest-density pockets of demand in any metro area. We deploy geo-targeted Google and Meta campaigns to those families during the college planning window — not before, not after.
